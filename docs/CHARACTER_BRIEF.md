# Character Brief

## Locked direction

- Female teenager, visually around 15–17.
- Everyday fashion, modern and approachable.
- Not chibi.
- Not strongly magical-girl or fantasy.
- Original design; do not imitate Winx or another franchise.
- Family-friendly and not sexualized.

## Proportions and pose

- Stylized fashion illustration, approximately 7–7.5 heads tall.
- Front view.
- Neutral standing pose.
- Arms slightly away from torso to expose clothing silhouettes.
- Feet parallel and balanced.
- One fixed pose/body template for MVP.

## Face and expression

- Friendly light smile.
- Slightly enlarged illustrated eyes, but not super-deformed anime.
- Clean facial shapes that will later support an optional face-photo mask.

## Rendering style

- Clean linework.
- Moderate detail.
- Soft cel shading.
- Bright, clean palette with varied item colors.
- Hair formed from readable large shapes rather than many tiny strands.

## Outfit tone

- T-shirt, blouse, shirt, cardigan, knitwear.
- Jeans, wide-leg pants, shorts, pleated and midi skirts.
- Casual dresses and light occasion dresses.
- Sneakers, flats, loafers and ankle boots.
- Beret, cap, headband, glasses, handbags and simple jewelry.
